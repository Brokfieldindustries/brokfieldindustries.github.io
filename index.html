About us

brokfieldindustries , based in Sialkot, Pakistan, have been manufacturers the fine quality leather clothing, leather gloves, leather handbags, leather purse, leather wallets, leather belts, different kinds of small leather goods and accessories, leather promotional items and leather fetish gear since 1977. Extensive research and many years of manufacturing experience have gone into every aspect of the design and quality control of our leather products, to ensure that any item bearing the "brokfieldindustries" label will provide decades of use in the different kinds of environments.
